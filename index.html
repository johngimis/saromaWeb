What We Do

Our long experience in the field makes us the best portal of the finest greek products to the rest of the world

  • Our Story

    For over a decade our company works in close contact with a wide network of excellent Greek Suppliers, selected according to the strictest criteria posed by our International Customers. We are specialized  in sourcing and selecting the best Greek Products, supporting local producers optimizing their production, implement quality standards with the relevant qualifications and Brand awareness  in order to meet exactly the specific criteria and requirements to successfully fulfill our International Clients' demands.  Featuring an Immediate response to our Retailers requests,  we select and submit a wide range of proposals to choose from and grant direct access to the manufacturers.  When you buy a product from our selected suppliers, you know that you are getting the best prices that the suppliers can offer you, based on your exact requirements.
